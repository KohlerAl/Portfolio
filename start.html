A few examples of my work and projects

Interactive installation for kids in the black forest

"Charlies Abenteuer" is a concept for an interactive installation for kids in the black forest. We follow Charlie the squirrel around the forest, teaching the children facts about the black forest and its inhabitants. The installation has physical signs for the different stations in the forest as well as a web app with quizzes and a score counter.

My job was the initial design and the coding of the web application. For that, I used JavaScript, HTML and CSS. The application was designed for phones since it was meant to be used in the forest.

Visual Novel: "A murder mystery"

This visual novel was made using TypeScript and Fudge, a game engine made by Furtwangen University. In this adventure, the player has to solve a murder, solving puzzles and gathering clues to come to the right conclusion. The player's decisions influence the story and the relationship to the characters, making it easier or harder to solve the murder. The assets and the coding, as well as the design, concept and story were made by me.

A world beyond

A world beyond was a project made with a friend. The protagonist gets trapped in a nightmare-ish dream world and it is the goal to find a way out of it. Aliens and other creatures inhabit the world, hiding the secret to return to the human realm.

My part in the development was the game design and the story development, creating a world full of mystery. The game was made with Blender and Unreal Engine.

Invertale

Invertale was made during a game jam in 2024. As a technical challenge, the game had to include procedural generation, while also fitting the theme "Inverted". We came up with the idea of invertale, where we join the protagonist solving puzzles in two different - inverted - worlds. The puzzles can only be solved by switching between the worlds to cross blockages and gain items.

My job during the game was the game design as well as the creating the assets for the game. The assets were made using aseprite and later added into Unity.

Morbus Animi

Morbus Animi was made in 2021 and 2022 as part of a project for University spanning two semesters. We follow Norah facing her nightmares and battle them step by step.

My job in the group was the game design as well as making part of the 3D Art. For the design it was decided to keep the surroundings very dark and moody and setting accents with the light. For the assets we chose a low poly style and I created them in Blender. Later, everything was added to Unity.

The witch's grimoire

The witch's grimoire was made as a protoype for my masters thesis. The goal was to figure out the learn effects of facts embedded into the story without making the learning part of the game obvious. In order to answer the research question, historical facts about the witch hunts were added to the story and game mechanics of the game, which was then presented to several participants. Each of them filled out the questionnaire before and after playing, to compare the learn effects. The user test showed an increase of both interest in and knowledge of the topic. The game was made with Aseprite to create the assets and Unity as the engine.

LED Stripes

This installation was made as part of a group project. It was the goal to utilize a total of seven LED strips and create an interactive installation. The user can manipulate the the visuals and sounds by moving their hands and making different gestures. The project was made in Touchdesigner.

Bobbys Adventure

Bobbys Adventure is an interacive installation made for two users. The goal is to cooperate to get bobby back to his nest and to safety. In order to do that, one user has to flap bobbys wings with either the space bar or by making sounds, while the other can make the leafes of the trees go away by blowing into the microphone.

The project was made using Unity and Blender.
